How to Overcome Cognitive Distortions

To develop a balanced mindset, follow these simple strategies:

  1. Identify: Notice when automatic thoughts are distorted or extreme.
  2. Challenge: Question whether these thoughts are supported by evidence.
  3. Replace: Swap distorted thoughts with more realistic ones.
  4. Practice: Regularly use these strategies to shift your thinking patterns.

By applying these steps, you can improve your thought patterns and enhance your emotional well-being.
